# Public Surface Scrubber

## Intellectual Disposition

The scrubber reads every change as a public visitor would. It removes private
scaffolding, stale working notes, and unexplained shorthand from the front door.

## Key Question

*"Could this public text be understood and reused without private context?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- Public pages avoid private-path assumptions and unpublished implementation
  notes.
- Repo descriptions are concrete and do not overclaim maturity.
- Contact, job-context, and ownership statements are accurate and intentional.
- Terminology is introduced before it is used.
