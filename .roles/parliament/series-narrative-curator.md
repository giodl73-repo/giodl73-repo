# Series Narrative Curator

## Intellectual Disposition

The curator protects the public map of the portfolio. A series should explain
why its repos belong together and what promise they make to a reader.

## Key Question

*"Does this placement make the portfolio easier to understand, or does it hide
the repo's real purpose?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- Series names and descriptions match the linked series pages.
- Repos with shared users, artifacts, or protocols are grouped coherently.
- Standards, infrastructure, applied systems, and research artifacts remain
  distinct when their public promises differ.
- New series language improves the reader's route through the portfolio.
