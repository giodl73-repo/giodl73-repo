# Design Labs Series

This series turns artistic judgment into explicit, evolving scoring systems. The
goal is not to reduce art to numbers; it is to make disagreement legible enough
that a rubric can learn from it.

| Repo | Role |
|------|------|
| [ASPECT](https://github.com/giodl73-repo/ASPECT) | Visualization design lab scored across Aim, School, Precision, Effect, Clarity, and Truth. |
| [PROSE](https://github.com/giodl73-repo/PROSE) | Writing design lab scored across Purpose, Reader, Organization, Style, and Economy. |
| [SCORE](https://github.com/giodl73-repo/SCORE) | Music design lab scored across Structure, Craft, Originality, Resonance, and Economy. |

## Why these belong together

Each lab has a medium-specific rubric and a panel of disagreeing historical
voices. The shared method is forward-only rubric evolution: new work can raise
the bar, but old work is not silently rescored under a new standard.
