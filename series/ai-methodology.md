# AI Methodology Series

This series contains the tools that make AI-first work disciplined instead of
vibes-driven: decisions become dated artifacts, and research ideas face a review
panel before they are treated as true.

| Repo | Role |
|------|------|
| [SIGNALS](https://github.com/giodl73-repo/SIGNALS) | Feature decision intelligence: skills produce dated evidence trails for product and engineering choices. |
| [PANEL](https://github.com/giodl73-repo/PANEL) | AI-simulated expert review for strengthening research before real submission or publication. |

## Why these belong together

SIGNALS captures what was decided and why. PANEL stress-tests whether the work is
credible. Together they are the methodology layer used by the other series.
